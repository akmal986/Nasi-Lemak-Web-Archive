4 Weekend Brunch Places in Kuala Lumpur

Brunch, an international obsession where breakfast and lunch are crossed into one meal, is probably part of many weekend itineraries. Typically served from late mornings to early afternoons, brunch options range from vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free meals to classics such as eggs benedict, hash browns and even, waffles!

While we can continue the long list of brunch foods, it is best you explore the menus yourself as cafes are constantly inventing new brunch items. As Kuala Lumpur has a respectable brunch scene, there is no shortage of places that serve up a delicious, filling and hearty meals to last you until dinner.

So, without further ado, here are our top four brunch places that have an amazing brunch menu that you will undoubtedly love.

Breakfast Thieves APW

A sister to the original café in Melbourne, Breakfast Thieves was brought over by three young Malaysians who stayed true to the industrial theme of the original architecture and decor. Offering a modern Australian breakfast, a brunch menu with an Asian twist, vegetarian as well as gluten free dishes options, there are various items to suit different diet restrictions. A must-try would be their “Mr. Terry Benedict”, which is a unique take on the classic Eggs Benedict!

For more information, visit the Breakfast Thieves website.

VCR Jalan Galloway

Located on Jalan Galloway, the cafe’s building was built in 1927 near the old Pudu Jail and is believed to have housed most of their wardens – hence the name Galloway. This cosy and relaxed café in the heart of Kuala Lumpur serves a Western-style brunch between 9am and 6pm. Check out their Scandinavian Breakfast with dill-cured salmon, or if you’re extra hungry, their hearty VCR Big Breakfast will surely fill you up!

For more information, visit the VCR website.

Mr. Chew’s Chino Latino Bar

Known for their quirky, cute food presentations, Mr. Chew’s Chino Latino Bar offers a journey in every course as inventive Asian delights infused with an international twist are served to you. Its chilled ambience with an all-you-can-have brunch every Sunday includes mains, soups and delicious desserts. You can order as much as you want from their eclectic menu within a three-hour window from 12pm to 3pm. We’ve heard their crisp and sweet Tempura Prawn Nori Taco, which consists of tempura battered nori sheets stuffed with sushi rice, succulent prawns and kimchi cream, is a crowd favourite.

For more information, visit Mr. Chew’s Chino Latino Bar website.

Serena Brasserie

Located in our hotel, Serena Brasserie hosts a brunch buffet between 12pm to 3pm where you can tuck into fresh seafood on ice, premium barbecue grill, live action stations, delicious desserts and more. Plus, buffets are the best brunch option if you’re going with a group of varying tastes! Lucky for you, we have Serena Brasserie's Buffet Deals for you to gift to your loved ones or treat yourself to our brunch buffet spread, lunch or even dinner!

Make your reservation for Serena Brasserie’s Sunday Brunch today.
